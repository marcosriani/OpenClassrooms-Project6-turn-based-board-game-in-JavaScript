Turn-based Board Game

For each turn, a player can move horizontally or vertically from one to three boxes.

To walk less then 3 blocks, you need to walk at least one block and click the End Turn button.

Each player begin with a bird weapon that can cause 10 points of damage.

If a player passes over a box containing a weapon, they leave their current weapon on site and replace it with the new one.

If players cross over adjacent squares (horizontally or vertically), a battle begins.

Each player attacks in turn.

As soon as the life points of a player (initially 100) falls to 0, they lose.

Click in the button above to go to the player area.
